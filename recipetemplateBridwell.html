Recipes by the Bridwells

Recipe Name

by Caleb Bridwell

Ingredients

  • ~3-4 lbs of chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup of white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce, or slightly more if you want more sauce
  • 4 cloves of garlic, diced or minced
  • 1 teaspoon of black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon of MSG (if you want it to be done right)
  • Steamed rice - NOT boiled rice

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients (except the rice) in pot or ziplock bag, let them marinate in refrigerator for 1-3 hours
  2. Boil the marinated ingredients in a pot over high heat
  3. Once boiling, reduce to medium/high heat, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes
  4. Remove the lid and stir ocassionally for an additional 20 minutes, allow the sauce to reduce and thicken
  5. Serve over the steamed rice
